# Managed Retainer (Security Operations)

## Monthly deliverables
- Cloudflare telemetry snapshot + scorecard (JSON + Markdown/PDF).
- Change log of infra changes (PRs, applies).
- Drift and control checks (state backend, CI integrity).
- Advisory tickets for remediation and improvements.

## What we manage
- Edge protections (Cloudflare rulesets, rate limits, custom WAF when enabled).
- Origin enforcement header and validation.
- Telemetry archive retention and lifecycle.
- CI/CD controls: OIDC, branch protection, environment approvals.

## Reporting cadence
- Weekly snapshot (optional) and monthly executive summary.
- Severity-based escalation for notable events.
